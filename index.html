The Lumen Lab at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) investigates fundamental research problems to enable efficient utilizations of AI techniques. In particular, we focus on learning and modeling with limited resources in terms of data, computing capability, as well as exploring their derivative applications on wide spectrums. Some specific topics include zero and few-shot learning, collaborative and federated learning, representation learning, model compression, and lightweight solutions of large foundation models. Prof. Jingcai Guo directs the Lumen Lab.

We are actively recruiting self-motivated PhD Students, Postdoctoral Researchers, and Research Assistants to work and have fun together on multiple research projects. Visiting Students/Researchers (onsite/remote) are also welcome!. If you are interested in joining the lab, please see the Join Us page.
